About NeoTech Forge

Learn more about our company and our mission to provide the best PC building experience.

Our Story

NeoTech Forge was founded in 2015 by a group of passionate PC enthusiasts who wanted to make the process of building a custom PC easier and more accessible to everyone.

What started as a small operation in a garage has grown into one of the leading custom PC builders in the country, with a team of over 50 dedicated employees and a state-of-the-art assembly facility.

Our mission is to provide gamers, content creators, and professionals with high-quality custom PCs that meet their specific needs and budgets. We believe that everyone deserves a PC that's built just for them, and we're committed to making that a reality.
